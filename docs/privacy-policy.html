ShopPilot Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

ShopPilot is designed with privacy first. We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal data on external servers. All data stays on your device.

1. Overview

ShopPilot is a Chrome browser extension that helps TikTok Shop sellers manage creator outreach, generate AI-powered content, and monitor competitor products. This privacy policy explains how ShopPilot handles your data.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

ShopPilot does not:

  • Collect any personal information (name, email, phone, etc.)
  • Track your browsing activity
  • Use analytics, cookies, or tracking pixels
  • Send any data to our servers or third-party services
  • Require user accounts or registration
  • Store any data outside of your browser

3. Data Stored Locally

ShopPilot stores the following data locally on your device using Chrome's built-in chrome.storage.local API:

Data TypePurposeLocation
API KeysAuthenticate with AI providers you choose (OpenAI, MiniMax, DeepSeek)Local storage only
Creator DataCache creator information from TikTok Seller Center for display in the extensionLocal storage only
Tracked ProductsStore competitor product info for price/sales monitoringLocal storage only
AI Generation HistoryKeep a local history of AI-generated messages and listings for your referenceLocal storage only
User PreferencesStore your settings (selected AI provider, model, language preferences)Local storage only

You can clear all locally stored data at any time by removing the extension or clearing its storage through Chrome's extension management page.

4. BYOK (Bring Your Own Key) Model

ShopPilot uses a Bring Your Own Key model for AI features. This means:

  • You provide your own API keys from AI providers (OpenAI, MiniMax, DeepSeek, or custom endpoints)
  • API calls are made directly from your browser to the AI provider you selected
  • ShopPilot never processes, proxies, or stores your API traffic
  • Your API key is stored locally and never transmitted to us

Please review the privacy policies of your chosen AI provider for how they handle data sent to their APIs.

5. Network Requests

ShopPilot makes network requests only in the following cases:

  • AI API calls: When you explicitly click "Generate" to create invite messages or listing copy. The request goes directly to the AI provider's API endpoint.
  • TikTok Seller Center pages: Content scripts run on TikTok Seller Center pages to read creator data and product information that is already loaded in your browser.

ShopPilot does not make any requests to our own servers. There are no update checks, telemetry pings, or background network activity beyond what is described above.

6. Permissions

ShopPilot requests the following Chrome permissions, each for a specific purpose:

PermissionWhy It's Needed

8. Children's Privacy

ShopPilot is designed for business use by TikTok Shop sellers. It is not directed at children under 13 years of age.
