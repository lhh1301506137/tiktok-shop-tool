Frequently Asked Questions

What is BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)?
BYOK means you provide your own AI API key (e.g., from OpenAI or DeepSeek). ShopPilot sends requests directly from your browser to the AI provider — we never see or store your key. This means lower cost for you and zero data exposure.
Which TikTok Shop regions are supported?
ShopPilot works with TikTok Seller Center in the US, UK, Singapore, Indonesia, Thailand, Malaysia, Vietnam, and Philippines. It also supports TikTok Affiliate Center globally.
Is my data safe?
Yes. ShopPilot stores everything in Chrome's local storage on your device. We have no servers, no databases, and no analytics. Your creator data, API keys, and browsing activity stay entirely on your machine.
Do I need a TikTok Shop seller account?
Yes, you need an existing TikTok Shop seller account. ShopPilot enhances the TikTok Seller Center interface — it works alongside the pages you already use to manage your shop.
Can I use a free AI API key?
Absolutely! MiniMax and DeepSeek offer generous free tiers. You can start generating AI content without spending a cent on API calls. OpenAI also offers free trial credits for new accounts.
